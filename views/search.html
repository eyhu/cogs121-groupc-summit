Help Documentation

The purpose of Summit is to allow users to freely share their experiences at different resorts and communicate with those who share the same interests. Users can decide a chat/community by specificying their skill levels and locations. Just simply start typing, leave your thoughts and click "Share" to post your comment.

If you like what others have said, you can also start a one-on-one private conversation.

If you have any other questions, please feel free to contact snow@summit.com.
